# ICE Trips
The observation that I was most controlled when the train was not delayed led me to the idea of tracking my daily ICE connection with additional information about the train, the reason for the delay, and whether my ticket was controlled or not.

## Properties
**date**
Day on which the record is captured

**first**
Initial station of the route

**last**
Final station of the route

**from**
Departure location for the specific segment

**to**
Arrival location for the specific segment

**train_number**
Operational identifier assigned to the train

**planned_departure**
Scheduled time when the train is intended to leave

**planned_arrival**
Scheduled time when the train is intended to arrive

**actual_departure**
Real time when the train leaves the station

**actual_arrival**
Real time when the train reaches the destination

**temperature**
Outside temperature in degree Celsius

**controlled**
Indicator showing that the record has been operationally verified

**delay_reason**
Documented cause for any deviation from the schedule
